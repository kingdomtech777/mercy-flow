✨ What We Do

At Mercy Flow, we don’t just preach the Gospel—we live it out. Our mission is to reach souls, connect them to the Body, baptize them into freedom, and disciple them to walk in their calling.

Evangelise

We share the Gospel through Spirit-led, Christ-centered outreach — engaging hearts, preaching truth, worshipping God, and inviting people into new life with Jesus.

“The harvest is great, but the workers are few.” – Matthew 9:37

Ministry methods

  • Street evangelism
  • Event-based outreach
  • One-on-one Gospel conversations
  • Distribution of tracts and resources

Connect

We help new believers find a spiritual home by working closely with a network of sound, Bible-teaching churches so those who encounter Jesus are nurtured and grown.

What we do

  • Match individuals with local churches
  • Maintain a vetted list of Spirit-filled, Bible-based churches
  • Follow up personally to ensure support

Find a church near you

Baptise

We lead people into freedom through water baptism — a public declaration of faith and a step into transformation and new life.

Our approach

  • Baptism preparation and teaching
  • Hosting regular baptism services
  • Facilitating deliverance and Holy Spirit filling

Disciple

We walk alongside new believers through mentoring, small groups, and teaching so they discover identity, gifts, and calling.

How we disciple

  • One-on-one mentoring
  • Small group discipleship
  • Biblical foundations teaching
  • Identity and calling workshops

Outcome

Mercy Flow exists to be His mercy in motion—not just telling the world about Jesus, but helping people live in His freedom, grow in His Spirit, and carry His message forward.
