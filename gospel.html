How to Respond: Repent and Believe

The Bible calls us to repent—to turn away from our sin and rebellion—and to put our trust in Jesus Christ alone for salvation.

Repentance means a genuine change of heart and mind about sin, recognizing that we can’t save ourselves and need God’s mercy.

Faith means trusting fully in Jesus—His death and resurrection—as the only way to be forgiven and made right with God.

Jesus said: “Repent and believe the good news!” (Mark 1:15)

A Simple Prayer You Can Pray

“Lord Jesus, I admit I am a sinner and need Your forgiveness. Thank You for dying for me and rising again. I turn from my sin and choose to follow You as my Savior and Lord. Please help me live for You from this day forward. Amen.”

If you prayed this sincerely, the Bible promises you have new life in Christ. If you have received Jesus Christ as Lord, let us know so we can help you on your journey.
